Brande Gilbert

CEO

Brande was born in Western Pennsylvania (outside of Pittsburgh) to a single mother. She has a sister, Anissa, who is one year older.

Brande had a feeling she was queer at a young age but suppressed those feelings due to insecurity and hateful religious rhetoric taught at the private schools she attended. Brande graduated from Edinboro University with a Bachelors of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Financial Services.

After being asked to co-lead a team in her employer's LGBTQIA+ group she recognized a passion for diversity, equity, and inclusion focusing specifically on the gay community and the Christian church.

Proud Proclamations, an apparel brand, was launched as a result of this. The brand seeks to spread meaningful, inclusive messaging regarding LGBTQIA+ issues, mental health, and issues impacting people of color.

Brande now resides in Atlanta, GA, where she enjoys being active by participating in CrossFit and going for long walks. She also likes to the take advantage of the social events Atlanta has to offer.
